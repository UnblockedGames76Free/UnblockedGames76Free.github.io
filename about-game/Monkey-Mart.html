Monkey Mart

What is Monkey Mart?

Game Concept

Monkey Mart is a fun game where you help a cute monkey run a supermarket! Plant fruits, pick them up, and fill the shelves with yummy food like bananas, corn, eggs, peanuts, coffee beans, chocolate, and more. Customers will grab items and wait for you to check them out at the register! As your store gets bigger, you can hire helpers and get machines to make cool stuff like chocolate bars, ice cream, popcorn, and yogurt. You can even give your monkey cool hats and improve their skills to make the market run better!

How to Play Monkey Mart

Run around the store and grab crops to fill up the shelves for your customers. Your monkey will do the job automatically when you're in the right spot!

  • Move: Use the WASD keys or the Arrow keys
