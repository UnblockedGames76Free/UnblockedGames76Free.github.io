Game Overview

In Vectaria.io, you begin your journey by choosing one of four characters: Martha, Oliver, Mike, or Rose. Once you're ready, jump straight into the multiplayer world with other players!

There are three exciting game modes:

  • PvP Survival Mode: Battle other players to become the strongest survivor.
  • Casual Survival Mode: Play survival mode with the option to toggle PvP on or off!
  • Creative Mode: Let your creativity shine as you build anything you can imagine!

With endless possibilities, every game in Vectaria.io is a new adventure. Which mode will you try first?

How to Play Vectaria.io

  • Movement: WASD or arrow keys
  • Place Block: Right mouse button
  • Mine / Attack: Left mouse button
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Open Inventory: X
  • Open Home Menu: G
  • View Spawn Points: M
  • Open Shop: O
  • Open Guide: I
  • Chat: Press enter, then type!

To toggle PvP mode, open your inventory (press 'X') and change it in the top left corner!

Tips to Succeed in Vectaria.io

  • Manage your inventory wisely and be ready to adapt to changes in the game.
